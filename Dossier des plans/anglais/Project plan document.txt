Project Name: My Shining Child
Project Start Date: 2023-07-11
Project Time Frame: 12 months
Project Languages: French-Canadian, English, Russian

Project Summary: The story follows Alexia, a 16-year-old girl who meets Ayana, a homeless 13-year-old. Alexia takes Ayana under her wing, grows attached, and decides to protect her by keeping her sheltered from the outside world. The narrative explores their evolving bond and the challenges Alexia faces in balancing her personal life and her role as Ayana's protector.
